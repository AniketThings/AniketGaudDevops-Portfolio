About Me

I am a DevOps Engineer with hands-on experience supporting a production, Australia-based e-commerce platform, where I work closely with development and business teams to ensure high availability, performance, and reliability of critical systems. My role involves designing, deploying, and maintaining scalable and secure cloud infrastructure on AWS, supporting both development and production environments. I specialize in end-to-end CI/CD automation, where I build and manage Jenkins pipelines to streamline application deployments, reduce manual effort, and accelerate release cycles while maintaining quality and stability. I actively work with containerized applications using Docker and deploy them on Kubernetes (EKS), handling real-world configurations such as services, ingress, secrets, autoscaling, and rolling updates. Infrastructure provisioning and environment consistency are achieved through Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using Terraform, enabling repeatable, version-controlled, and cost-optimized cloud setups. I follow security best practices, including IAM-based role access, bastion host architecture, and least-privilege policies, to ensure secure operations in production environments. In addition to deployments, I am deeply involved in production monitoring and incident handling, using tools like Prometheus and Grafana to track system health, resource utilization, and application performance. I regularly troubleshoot production issues such as pod failures, pipeline errors, scaling issues, and service outages, ensuring minimal downtime and fast recovery. I strongly believe in automation, observability, and continuous improvement, and I enjoy collaborating with development teams to improve deployment speed, system reliability, and overall engineering efficiency. My goal is to build resilient cloud-native systems that can scale seamlessly while maintaining security, performance, and cost efficiency.
